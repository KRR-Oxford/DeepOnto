deeponto.evaluation.eval_metrics.f1(pred: Iterable, ref: Iterable, null_ref: Optional[Iterable] = None)[source]

Semantics-aware F1 score when precision and recall are equally important, i.e.: F1 = 2 * P * R / (P + R).

deeponto.evaluation.eval_metrics.f_score(pred: Iterable, ref: Iterable, beta: float, null_ref: Optional[Iterable] = None)[source]

Semantics-aware F-score with factor β where elements in the {null_set} should be ignored, i.e.: neither +ve nor -ve.

Check concepts at: https://lawhy.github.io//pythagorean-means/.
